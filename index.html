What Sets IPS Apart from the Rest

Best Fit Schools

IPS school have a variety of instructional models. This provides staff the opportunity to find the best fit school for their instructional philosophy. The district has a variety of models such as STEM, Arts, Montessori, Inquiry Based, Traditional School models, to name a few, allowing staff to find a perfect fit for their instructional practice.

Ability to Impact

IPS believes strongly in the potential of every student regardless of their background or income. We also recognize the unique challenges of working in an urban area and are committed to providing teachers with the supports they need to make a positive, lasting impact on students. Because of this our staff members are committed to impacting all students, and providing teachers the support they need to make a difference.

Opportunity to Grow

While the district continues to invest in our compensation model, we have also made significant progress developing additional compensation structures that allow teachers to expand their impact, grow as professionals and remain in the classroom. District Teacher Leaders play an important role in giving central office leadership feedback on topics including evaluation, curriculum and instruction.

Teaching at IPS: It's All Here!

Indianapolis Public Schools is the place to launch a career in urban education. The district's investment in Teacher Leadership allows teachers to hold leadership positions while still remaining in the classroom. With multiple instructional models, staff members have the opportunity to find a school that is the best fit for them. Our staff serves students and families from the heart of Indianapolis, giving them the greatest opportunity to impact students and families.
